Interactive Standards

In general:
  No needless clutter.
  Strive for clarity without text

Ordinary Text:  Black
Labels:  Color of object or black

Points:  
  Color: Black
  Size: ggb 3
  
Moveable Points
  Shape: Circle
  Color: red-primary,  blue-secondary
  Size:  ggb 3
  
Sliders
  Point style: 4
  Line thickness: 5
  
Font:
  Size: 12
  Text: small
  Latex labeling, i.e. serif 
  
Angles and distances
  line weight: 2
  color: black
  no fill

Axis:     Black,  triangular arrowhead


Grid:
  Color 1/8 Gray #E0E0E0
  Major Gridlines only

Force Vectors:  
  Primary / applied force:  Red
  Secondary / reactions / alternate representation : Blue
  Resultants: Black
  ggb line thickness 5 (for 2D), 3 for (for 3D)
  
Moments:
  Circular Arrows  Red
  Moment Vectors  Black
  
Objects
  stroke: 5
  fill:   black 25% transparent
