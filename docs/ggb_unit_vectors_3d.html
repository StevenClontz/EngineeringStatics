Engineering Statics

This interactive allows you to input two points, \(A\) and \(B\text{,}\) and then will show the position vector \(\vec{AB}\) connecting \(A\) to \(B\text{.}\) Additionally, the interactive will compute a unit vector \(\widehat{AB}\) and force vector \(\vec{F}_{AB}\) along the same line. By unchecking the answer boxes, you can input various vector endpoints and practice your computations of position vectors, unit vectors, and force vectors. Note that you can click on the 3D drawing to adjust the viewing perspective and also that GeoGebra displays vectors components as a column vector,

\begin{equation*} \vec{A} = \begin{pmatrix}A_x\\A_y\\A_z\end{pmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
