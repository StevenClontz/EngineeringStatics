Points are specified with these three cylindrical coordinates.

  • \(r\text{,}\) the distance from the origin to the projection of the tip of the vector onto the \(xy\) plane,

  • \(\theta\text{,}\) the angle, measured counterclockwise from the positive \(x\) axis to the projection of the vector onto the \(xy\) plane

  • \(z\text{,}\) the vertical height of the vector tip.
