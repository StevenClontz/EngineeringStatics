If you do need to find the vector representation of the moment about a line, then you simply need to multiply your scalar magnitude times the unit vector of the line of interest. For the example involving line \(DE\text{,}\) you would multiply the magnitude of \(\vec{M}_{DE}\) by the unit vector \(\vec{u}_{DE}\)| (pure direction) to find the vector components of \(\vec{M}_{DE}\text{.}\)]
