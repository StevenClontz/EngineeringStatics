To emphasize that unit vectors are pure direction, track what happens when a vector is divided by its magnitude

\begin{equation*} \text{unit vector} = \frac{\vec{F}}{| \vec{F} | } = \frac{\text{[vector]}}{\text{[magnitude]}}=\frac{\cancel{\text{[magnitude]}} \cdot\text{[direction]}}{\cancel{\text{[magnitude]}}} = \text{[direction]} \text{.} \end{equation*}
