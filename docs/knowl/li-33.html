Item 2.
Associative. It does not matter whether you multiply a scalar value a by the final dot product, or either of the individual vectors, you will still get the same answer.
\begin{equation} a\left( \vec{A}\cdot\vec{B} \right ) = a\vec{A}\cdot\vec{B}= \vec{A}\cdot a\vec{B}\tag{2.4} \end{equation}
