To calculate the value of the mixed triple product, evaluate the determinant of a matrix consisting of the components of the unit vector \(\hat{u}_{DE}\) along the line of interest \(DE\) in the top row, the components of a position vector \(\vec{r}\) from line-of-interest \(DE\) to the line-of-action of force \(\vec{F}\) in the middle row, components of the force vector \(\vec{F}\) in the bottom row.
