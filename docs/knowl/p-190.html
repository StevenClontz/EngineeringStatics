In spherical coordinates, points are specified with these three coordinates
  • \(r\text{,}\) the distance from the origin to the tip of the vector,
  • \(\theta\text{,}\) the angle, measured counter-clockwise from the positive \(x\) axis to the projection of the vector onto the \(xy\) plane, and
  • \(\phi\text{,}\) the polar angle from the \(z\) axis to the vector.
