Imagine you have a pool which is \(h\) meters deep. At the top of the water, the gage pressure is zero no matter which unit system you use. As you descend to the bottom of the pool, the fluid pressure \(P\) increases with depth according to the equation \(P = \rho g h\text{,}\) where:

  • \(\rho\) is the density of a fluid,
  • \(g\) is gravitational acceleration, and
  • \(h\) is the height of fluid above the point of interest.
