Interactive Diagram.

The inteactive calculates the moment of inertia of a semi-circle about three different axes using the parallel axis theorem \(I = \bar{I} + A d^2\text{.}\) The distance term \(d\) represents the distance between the axis of interest and a parallel axis passing through the centroid of the shape.

Move the red dots to change the size and location of the semi-circle.
