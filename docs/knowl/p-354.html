This interactive shows the vector projection of \(\vec{A}\) on \(\vec{B}\) or of \(\vec{B}\) on \(\vec{A}\text{.}\)

You may change \(\vec{A}\) and \(\vec{B}\) by moving the red dots. Click dot to switch between \(x\)-\(y\) mode and \(z\) mode. Coordinates of \(\vec{A}\) and \(\vec{B}\) can also be entered into the table.
