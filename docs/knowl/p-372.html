The cross product of two three-dimensional vectors can be calculated by evaluating the determinant of this \(3 \times 3\) matrix.

\begin{equation} \vec{A} \times \vec{B} = \begin{vmatrix} \ihat \amp \jhat \amp \khat \\ A_x \amp A_y \amp A_z \\ B_x \amp B_y \amp B_z \end{vmatrix} \tag{2.8.3} \end{equation}
