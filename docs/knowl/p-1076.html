To find what you need on the final so that you average grade \(\bar{G}\) is a 70

\begin{align*} \bar{G} \amp= \frac{\sum G_i w_i}{\sum w_i}\\ 70 \amp = \frac{(0.25 \times 40) + (0.25 \times 80) + (0.1 \times 90) + (0.4 \times FE)}{(0.25 + 0.25 + 0.1 + 0.4 )}\\ FE \amp = \frac{70 (1) - (10 +30 + 9)}{0.4} = 77.5 \text{.} \end{align*}
