This rotational tendency is known as the moment of the force, or more simply the moment, and it is a vector quantity. You may be familiar with the term torque from physics. Engineers use “torque” where physicists use “moment” to describe the same concept. Moments are computed around a specific point and we use the notation \(\vec{M}_A\) to indicate the axis of rotation is point \(A\text{.}\) The \(\vec{M}\) is bold it represents a vector.
