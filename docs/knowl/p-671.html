Equations (4.2.1) and (4.3.1) not only produce the same result, they are completely identical. If the length of the handle is \(d\) and the angle between the force \(\vec{F}\) and the handle is \(\theta\text{,}\) then \(d_\perp = d \sin \theta\) and \(F_\perp = F \sin \theta\text{.}\) Using either equation to calculate the moment gives

\begin{equation} M = F\ d\ \sin \theta\text{.}\tag{4.3.2} \end{equation}
