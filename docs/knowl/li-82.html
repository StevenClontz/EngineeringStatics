Item 3.

Find \(\widehat{\vec{AB}}\text{,}\) the unit vector from \(A\) to \(B\text{,}\) by dividing vector \(\vec{AB}\) by its magnitude. This is a unitless vector with a magnitude of 1 which points from \(A\) to \(B\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \widehat{\vec{AB}}= \left \langle \frac{A_x}{|A|},\frac{A_y}{|A|},\frac{A_z}{|A|} \right \rangle \end{equation*}
