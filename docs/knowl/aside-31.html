Interactive Diagram.

Interactive shows the moment \(\vec{M} \) about an arbitrary point \(A\) due to a force force is defined by a scalar \(F\) and two points on its line of action, \(P_1\) and \(P_2\text{.}\)

The locations of \(A\text{,}\) \(P_1\text{,}\) \(P_2\) and the magnitude of \(\vec{F}\) can be set interactively. The interactive determines the components of \(\vec{r}\text{,}\) the unit vector \(\lambda\) of the line of action, the components of \(\vec{F}\) and the moment \(\vec{M}\) and its magnitude \(M\text{.}\)
