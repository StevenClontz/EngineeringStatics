Item 4. Identify all reactions.

Traverse the perimeter of the object and wherever a support was removed when isolating the body, replace it with the forces and/or couple-moments which it provides. Label each reaction with a descriptive variable name and a clear arrowhead. Again, if a vector’s direction is unknown just assume one.

The reaction forces and moments provided by common two-dimensional supports are shown in Figure 5.3.1 and three dimensional support in Figure 5.3.2. Identifying the correct reaction forces and couple-moments coming from supports is perhaps the most challenging step in the entire equilibrium process.
