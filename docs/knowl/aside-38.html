Instructions.

This interactive allows you to change the cut location on a \(\m{10}\) long beam with a downward distributed load varying from \(\Nperm{10}\) at point \(A\) to \(\Nperm{0}\) at point \(B\text{.}\) The check box shows the reaction forces, the equivalent loads, and exposes the internal loadings. Moving point \(C\) traces out the shear and moment as functions of \(x\text{.}\)
