Solution 2.7.2.1.
\begin{align*} \cos \theta \amp = \frac{\ihat \cdot \jhat}{ | \ihat | | \jhat |}\\ \amp = \frac{(1)(0) + (0)(1) + (0)(0)}{(1)(1)}\\ \amp = 0 \\ \\ \theta \amp = \cos^{-1}(0) \\ \amp = \ang{90} \end{align*}

This shows that \(\ihat\) and \(\jhat\) are perpendicular to each other.
