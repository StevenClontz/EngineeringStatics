Key Questions 8.

At the end of this chapter you should be able to answer these questions.

  • Identify the three basic internal loadings in two-dimensional systems.
  • Articulate the standard sign convention for internal forces.
  • Develop free body diagrams that include internal loads.
  • Use static equilibrium to calculate the unknown internal loadings at a single point on a rigid body.
  • Articulate the mathematical relationship between load, shear, and moment in beams.
  • Construct shear and bending moment diagrams using multiple approaches.
