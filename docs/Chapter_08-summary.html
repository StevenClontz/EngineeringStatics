Section 8.10 Summary

You have likely realized that in engineering (and life) that there are multiple ways to solve a problem. The four different techniques to compute internal loadings discussed in this chapter are a demonstration of this. In the end, the choice of which method to use is yours; the better you know each method the easier it will be to choose the one which is most applicable and efficient.
