Section 1.3 Chapter 4

Interactive Diagram.

This interactive shows how a force \(\vec{F}\) causes a moment \(\vec{M}_A\) around point \(A\text{.}\) Rotate the the wrench and force \(\vec{F}\) to see how the magnitude of the moment changes.

Interactive Diagram.

This interactive shows \(d_\perp\text{,}\) the perpendicular distance between the center of rotation and the line of action of the force. The moment is the force times the perpendicular distance.

Interactive Diagram.

This interactive shows the force applied to the wrench broken into components perpendicular and parallel to the handle. The moment is the perpendicular component times the length of the handle.

Interactive Diagram.

This interactive shows the force and position vectors for use in the moment cross product. The position vector is a vector from the moment center to any point on the line of action of the force.

Interactive Diagram.

This interactive demonstrates three methods to visualize and compute the two-dimensional moment of a force about point.

Interactive Diagram.

Interactive shows \(\vec{M} = \vec{r} \times \vec{F}\text{.}\) Vectors \vec{F} and \vec{r} can be adjusted interactively by dragging or changing the values in the table. Moment \vec{M} is calculated with the cross product automatically.

Interactive Diagram.

Interactive shows the moment \(\vec{M} \) about an arbitrary point \(A\) due to a force force is defined by a scalar \(F\) and two points on its line of action, \(P_1\) and \(P_2\text{.}\)

The locations of \(A\text{,}\) \(P_1\text{,}\) \(P_2\) and the magnitude of \(\vec{F}\) can be set interactively. The interactive determines the components of \(\vec{r}\text{,}\) the unit vector \(\lambda\) of the line of action, the components of \(\vec{F}\) and the moment \(\vec{M}\) and its magnitude \(M\text{.}\)

Interactive Diagram.

In this interactive you can adjust the moment center \(A\text{,}\) the direction of the equal and opposite forces, and the distance between the lines of action. The net moment of the couple only depends on \(F\) and the distance between the parallel lines. \(M = F d_\perp\text{.}\)

Interactive Diagram.

The interactive shows an object loaded with two forces, \(\vec{A}\) and \(\vec{B}\text{,}\) and a couple-moment \(\vec{C}\) along with the equivalent resultant force \(\vec{R}\) and moment \(\vec{M}_R\text{.}\)

Interactive Diagram.

Interactive shows the projection of moment \(\vec{M}\) on line through \(A\) and \(B\) with unit direction vector \(\hat{u}\text{.}\) The projection of \(M\) on line \(AB\) is (\(M \cdot \hat{u}) \hat{u}\text{.}\)

Interactive Diagram.

Interactive shows the moment produced by pushing on a door handle with a force \(\vec{F}\text{,}\) and the portion of that moment along the door hinge axis, \(\vec{M}_z\text{.}\)
